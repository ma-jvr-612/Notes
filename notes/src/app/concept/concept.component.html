How Hestia Works Create Your Recipes Step 1 Build a collection of your favorite recipes and their ingredients. Whether it's stir-fried rice, pasta carbonara, or homemade pizza, create each recipe once and reuse it forever.

Each recipe becomes a template that you can quickly add to any grocery list with just one click. Build Your Grocery Lists Step 2 Create grocery lists for your shopping trips and instantly add your saved recipes to them. No more searching for ingredient lists online or rewriting the same items over and over.

Planning to cook stir-fried rice this week? Just tap to add the recipe to your list, then add any other items you need. It's that simple. Shop with Ease Step 3 Use the mobile view to check off items as you shop. Your lists sync automatically, making grocery shopping faster and more organized.

Hestia keeps your recipes and lists in one place, saving you time and making meal planning effortless.
